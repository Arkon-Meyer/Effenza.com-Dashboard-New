# Security, Privacy & Compliance Milestones (MVP → GA)

## Phase 1 – MVP Readiness (Security Foundations)
- Implement RBAC and MFA for all privileged accounts
- Encrypt all data at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- Basic immutable logging (Azure Blob or equivalent)
- Publish GDPR-compliant privacy policy
- Define initial QMS framework (ISO 9001)
- Update privacy notice to include CPRA rights

## Phase 2 – Enhanced Compliance
- Automated account deactivation for inactive users
- Key rotation and HSM-backed key storage
- User-facing activity logs
- Formal CAPA process (ISO 9001)
- Opt-out mechanism for CPRA “Do Not Sell or Share” requests
- Right to be forgotten & data export functionality
- Supplier/vendor quality evaluation procedure

## Phase 3 – Audit-Ready Status
- Centralized logging with SIEM integration
- Documented DPIA process
- Internal quality audits & management review meetings (ISO 9001)
- Customer feedback resolution tracking
- CPRA-sensitive information usage limitation mechanism
- Maintain CPRA request log
- Full retention policy enforcement for all data categories

## Phase 4 – Enterprise / S&P 500 Alignment
- Evidence of periodic security & quality audits
- Demonstrable KPI improvements in quality metrics
- CPRA & GDPR compliance reports ready for due diligence
- Supplier compliance verification for ISO 9001, GDPR, and CPRA
- Formal incident response test report
